About Me

Hi, I’m Justin, an environmental scientist and EHS specialist based in Racine, Wisconsin. I work for Arcadis, a global consulting firm, where I focus on environmental health and safety solutions. My educational background is in geology, having studied at Southern Illinois University and the University of Nevada. More recently I have expanded my credentails to Health and Safety and Environmental Compliance with certification as a ISO 14001 Lead Auditor, and Associate Safety Professional. I also have training in software development from Actualize Coding Bootcamp. Outside of work, I’m passionate about the outdoors and technology. I enjoy playing video games, tennis, and camping, as well as watching fantasy and horror movies. I’m married to my wonderful wife, Rebeca, a nutrition educator. And I also have a top-notch dog, a Golden Retriever, Dobber, who is seven. Thanks for stopping by! Feel free to connect with me.
