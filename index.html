About Me

Hi, I’m Justin, an environmental scientist and EHS specialist based in the Chicago area. My educational background is in geology, having studied at Southern Illinois University and the University of Nevada. Since then, I've expanded into health, safety, and environmental compliance, earning certifications as an ISO 14001 Lead Auditor and Associate Safety Professional. Somewhere along the way, I also decided computers weren't complicated enough, so I completed software development training through Actualize Coding Bootcamp.

When I'm not helping keep workplaces safer or convincing chemicals to stay where they're supposed to, you'll usually find me outdoors or tinkering with technology. I enjoy video games, tennis, camping, and just about anything involving maps or gadgets. I'm also a fan of fantasy and horror movies, because apparently my entertainment preferences can't decide whether they want dragons or demons.

I'm married to the amazing Rebeca, a nutrition educator who patiently tolerates my annual (or more frequent) rewatches of The Lord of the Rings trilogy. We also have a nine-year-old Golden Retriever named Dobber, who firmly believes his full-time job is supervising the household and reminding us when it's dinner time.
